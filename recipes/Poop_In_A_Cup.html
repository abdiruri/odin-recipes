POOP In A Cup

Description:

Its POOP in cup. Well not really but it looks like it kinda.

Ingredients to make the POOP:

  • 1 1/4 c chocolate chips
  • 1 pkg marshmallows
  • 1 stick butter
  • 6 c rice krispies
  • 1 1/4 c peanut butter
  • 1/2 c walnuts
  • 1/2 c raisins
  • 1/2 c miniature marshmallows

Steps to make the POOP:

  1. Melt butter in a large sauce non stick pan over low-medium heat. add the bag of marshmallows and stir until completely mixed and smooth. Stir over the 6 cups of rice krispies. Mix extremely well and quickly.
  2. Take a handful of the mixture and press into a cupcake pan molding a cup shape. I use a plastic easter egg to press into the middle to smooth it out and make it deeper than my fingers can.
  3. Let these harden for an hour.
  4. In the cleane,d same non stick sauce pan, add the peanut butter and chocolate chips, over low to medium heat and stir until conbined and melted smooth
  5. Mix in the walnuts, raisins and mini marshmallows. Stir until covered and coated well. Using a regular eating table spoon, scoop enough of the mixture into the rice krispie cups to fill without pouring over the sides.
  6. Put them in the fridge for about 1 hour and enjoy. Maybe.
