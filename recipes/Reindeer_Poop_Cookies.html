Reindeer POOP Cookies

Description:

I need to stop. Anyways here is another POOP recipe.

Ingredients to make the POOP:

  • chocolate chips
  • caramels
  • 5 ounces marshmallows
  • butter
  • vanilla extract
  • crispy rice cereal

Steps to make the POOP:

  1. First, in a large saucepan over medium-low heat, melt the butter and caramel squares, stirring frequently, until smooth.
  2. Stir in the marshmallows, chocolate chips, and vanilla, continuing to stir until fully melted and smooth.
  3. Remove the pan from the heat and stir in the crispy rice cereal until evenly coated.
  4. Use a cookie scoop (or a tablespoon) to drop the mixture onto a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper or wax paper and press the mixture into neat little piles, if needed.
  5. Let set at room temperature (or move to the fridge to speed up the process) and then enjoy! Or not.
